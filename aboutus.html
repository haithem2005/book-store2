About Us

Bookstore was founded in December 2019 by 5 enthusiastic developers: Worod Tahmaz, Deepthy Nair, Avani Agrawal, Haithem Elhessawy and Mariana Serna in a cozy town named Lund located at the south of Sweden.

Our selection of more than 20,000 titles ranges from the classics of world literature to the cutting edge of contemporary fiction and poetry, not forgetting a copious display of history, politics, philosophy, cookery, essays and children’s books. And our lovely online shop, provides the perfect setting in which to explore them all.

Our aim has always been to represent on our catalogue the distinctive ethos of the Review – intelligent without being pompous; engaged without being partisan. If you aren’t able to visit us in person, we hope our website will give you a flavour of what makes the shop unique. If you are able to join us in Bury Place, we look forward to finding you the books you know you need, and more importantly, the ones you didn’t know you needed.
